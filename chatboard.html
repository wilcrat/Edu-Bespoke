Who is a Scientist?
A scientist is someone who engages in systematic and methodical inquiry to expand our understanding of the natural world, including living organisms.
What does a Scientist do?
Formulating hypotheses: proposing explanations for observations or questions.
Conducting experiments: testing those hypotheses through controlled observations and data collection.
